Isaiah Decoded

Isaiah is not a sealed book. Here is how to open it.

Avraham Gileadi's typological framework reveals that Isaiah wrote in layers of symbolic code — and that his message is for our day. The keys below will transform Isaiah from an impenetrable ancient text into a living message addressed directly to you.

Isaiah wrote in symbolic language. "Babylon" = worldliness. "Arm of the Lord" = God's servant. Learn the cipher and the text transforms.

  1. Isaiah 48-49 — The Master Key (read with 1 Nephi 20-21 annotations)
